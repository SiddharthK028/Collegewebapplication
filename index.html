About us

Welcome to
SKSVMACET

Smt. Kamala and Sri Venkappa M. Agadi College of Engineering & Technology (SKSVMACET) is an Engineering and Technology college, spread over 60 acres of greenery in Lakshmeshwar, Karnataka, India.

The college is approved by AICTE and Affiliated to V. T. U., Belgaum and Government of Karnataka.

The primary focus of the institute is to help develop competent and knowledgeable Engineers in various disciplines capable of competing and excelling in an increasingly dynamic global community.
